Reine Des Glaces



Profile


Botanical name: Lactuca sativa

Family: Asteraceae

Hardiness: Frost-tolerant annual

Approximate Maturity (Soil): 66-80 Days

Approximate Maturity (Hydroponics): 28-35 Days

Description: Crisp, semi-wrapped tight head Batavia lettuce. Provides a crisp texture with toothed leaves. The name translates to 'Queen (Reine) of (Des) Ice (Glaces)' due to its ability to tolerate frost.

Care: Keep well watered and in partial shade. For plants grown hydroponically - pick outer leaves for continous harvest

Soil Type: Moist, well prepared soil or compost.
